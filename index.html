Welcome to Your Neighborhood Grocer

McKenney's Market is more than just a grocery store; it's a community hub dedicated to providing the freshest produce, highest-quality meats, and local artisan goods. Founded by Scott McKenney, our commitment to quality ensures that every item on your list meets our rigorous standards for freshness and taste. We look forward to serving you!

The McKenney Quality Promise

We partner directly with local farms and suppliers to reduce food mileage and bring you products at their peak flavor. Our promise is transparency: we believe you should know where your food comes from, and we take pride in supporting the local economy with every sale. Stop by and taste the difference that local sourcing makes!

Store Hours & Location

We are open Monday - Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Find us at 123 Main Street, Anytown.
